# Video Recording Checklist

Use this checklist before recording a portfolio or product showcase video for Growth Strategy Copilot.

## Before Recording

- Open the production URL: <https://growth-strategy-copilot.vercel.app>
- Click **Reset Demo** to start from a clean browser-local state.
- Use English mode first.
- Set browser zoom to 90% or 100%.
- Hide the bookmarks bar if needed.
- Close unrelated tabs and desktop applications.
- Use a 1440px or 1280px browser width.
- Check microphone input if recording voiceover live.
- Confirm no console errors are visible.
- Review the sample data limitation and mention it in the narration.

## During Recording

- Keep cursor movement slow and deliberate.
- Do not scroll too fast.
- Pause briefly on Diagnosis, Strategy, Experiments, and Report.
- Show the synthetic data limitation.
- Show the English / Chinese switch near the end.
- Avoid opening developer tools during the main recording.
- Keep the product narrative focused on workflow, evidence, and decision support.

## After Recording

- Trim dead time at the beginning and end.
- Add short captions if needed.
- Export at 1080p.
- Keep the final video under 3 minutes.
- Review audio clarity.
- Confirm that text is readable on the final export.
- Test playback before sharing.
- Save a short 60-second cut if the video will be used for a social or portfolio preview.
