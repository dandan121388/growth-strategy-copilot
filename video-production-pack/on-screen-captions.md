## Placement Tips

- Use one caption per scene.
- Keep each caption on screen for 3-5 seconds.
- Avoid covering metric cards, strategy scores, or report headings.
- Use consistent typography across the video.
- Prefer subtle SaaS-style overlays over animated stickers or decorative effects.
