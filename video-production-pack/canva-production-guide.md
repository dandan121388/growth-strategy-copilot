# Canva Production Guide

Use this workflow to create a presentation-style product demo video with screenshots, short recordings, captions, and AI voiceover.

## 1. Prepare Materials

Collect:

- A short screen recording of the full product flow.
- Screenshots of Dashboard, Diagnosis, Segmentation, Strategy, Experiments, and Report.
- The script from `full-demo-script-en.md` or `full-demo-script-zh.md`.
- Captions from `on-screen-captions.md`.
- Optional subtitles from `subtitles-en.srt` or `subtitles-zh.srt`.

## 2. Create the Canva Video

1. Open Canva and create a 1920 x 1080 video project.
2. Add a clean title slide.
3. Add screen recordings or screenshots scene by scene.
4. Use simple transitions such as fade or slide.
5. Avoid decorative effects that make the product feel less professional.

## 3. Add AI Voiceover

Options:

- Use Canva's text-to-speech tools if available.
- Generate voiceover in another AI voice tool and upload the audio.
- Match audio sections to each scene manually.

Recommended style:

- Calm
- Clear
- SaaS product walkthrough tone
- Medium pace

## 4. Add Captions and Labels

1. Add short scene captions from `on-screen-captions.md`.
2. Keep captions in the same position and style across slides.
3. Add subtitles if the video will be watched without sound.
4. Use high-contrast text.

## 5. Suggested Scene Flow

1. Product title and purpose.
2. Dashboard and demo setup.
3. Diagnosis and bottleneck.
4. User segmentation.
5. Strategy priority matrix.
6. Experiment design.
7. Executive report.
8. Bilingual localization.
9. Closing URL.

## 6. Export

- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1080p
- Recommended duration: 2-3 minutes
- Short version: 45-60 seconds
- Filename example: `growth-strategy-copilot-product-demo.mp4`

## Final Review

- Check that UI text is readable.
- Check that captions do not cover important metrics.
- Confirm the video mentions synthetic data and mock AI.
- Confirm the video does not claim real business outcomes.
- Watch the final MP4 once before sharing.
