# Short Demo Script - English

Recommended length: 45 to 60 seconds.

Use case: LinkedIn, portfolio preview, or quick product walkthrough.

Growth Strategy Copilot is an AI-assisted growth diagnosis workspace for subscription-based businesses.

Instead of using a generic chatbot, the product guides the user through a structured analysis flow: business setup, sample data, metric diagnosis, user segmentation, strategy prioritization, experiment design, and executive reporting.

This demo uses synthetic fitness subscription data and mock AI. The goal is to demonstrate the workflow, not to claim real business inference.

The rule engine calculates metrics, detects bottlenecks, assigns user segments, and scores strategy priority. The AI layer is designed for interpretation, communication, and report generation.

P0 and P1 strategies are converted into A/B test plans with target users, metrics, guardrails, and decision rules.

The final report includes an executive summary, detailed analysis, presentation outline, and English / Chinese localization.

Growth Strategy Copilot demonstrates how AI can support business analysis while keeping analytical decisions transparent and reviewable.
